What is Norbert's Gambit?

Norbert's Gambit is a technique for converting money between CAD and USD that is more cost-efficient than using rates provided by brokers or the bank.

Converting CAD to USD, the technique is performed by buying a CAD-denominated USD ETF (DLR) with CAD funds, journalling it to the USD-denominated equivalent ETF (DLR-U), then selling shares to obtain USD funds.

Norbert's Gambit can be executed to convert USD funds to CAD by buying DLR-U (USD), journalling it to DLR (CAD), and selling DLR to obtain CAD funds.

The process of "journalling" DLR to DLR-U, or vice versa, is different depending on the bank or brokerage being used.
